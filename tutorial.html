FLAPPY KATS: ADVENTURE ZERO Introduction. FlappyKatz is a fun easy game where you help our hero player survive by constant screen clicks and avoiding obstacles. There's no messy task of dressing the player or tasking a constant violent kill. Its just plain good ole survival game. Goal The goal is simple-gain as much points as possible, survive as long as long as you can, get the crystal stones to hurdle the level. There are 5 existing level at the moment and future version shall be expanded later. Flying Technique: We have demo training page that provides 90 seocnds game tutorial. Chekc it out. Flying is as easy as clicking the screen. Three Hearts Rule The heart provides 3 lives till retry button surface. There's enough time to play as long as you stay alive and catches more and more falling items. After each death, Click Retry Button to re-spawn again. You can spend up to 3 hearts in our gameplay. Click GameOver Continue Button to repeat the game. Point Systems The game has point system embedded. a. Obstacle Pass 1 point. b. Bananas 5 points c. Bomb hits: 1: msg = "Damaged! Health dropped to 50%" Points dropped 50 percent. 2: msg = "Damaged! Health critical! 25% Left" Points has reduced to 25 percent. 3: msg = "Sorry 100% damaged! You are dead meat!" Instant Kill for the player. Game Over. d. Red Potion - minus 5 points. e. Green Potion (15 ), Blue Potion (10 ) f. Crystal Stones - 1,000 points Leaderboard Show up after level complete. You may access it after gameplay by clicking leaderboard button on the lower portion of the app in the level menu selection. Global List displays the top player workdwide, Weekly list provides listing for the week. Daily List provides daily top ranking. Crystal Stone Myth You need to gather 3 rare crystal stones and after capture , crystal stone will be displayed on upper left corner.
